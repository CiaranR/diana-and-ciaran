Michelle Altmann
Maid of Honor

Michelle is Diana's little sister and best friend, even though she thinks Diana is "so old" and "boring" sometimes. Michelle always manages to make Diana laugh, even when she is trying to give big-sister advice (aka being bossy).

Darren Rooney
Best Man

Darren is Ciarán's younger brother. He has long suffered from middle child syndrome and is now looking forward to his chance to shine. He has promised not to download his speech from the internet.

Rachel Willms
Bridesmaid

Rachel is Diana's oldest friend, they have known each other their whole lives and grew up down the block from each other. They spent their childhood together riding bikes, going to the beach, and playing tennis with the dream of one day making it to Wimbledon. Diana has, but had to pay for the ticket.

Ultan Rooney
Groomsman

Ultan is Ciarán's little brother. As the youngest in the family, he is used to getting things his own way. Now he will have to listen to what his big brother Diana says for once.

Christine Valentic
Bridesmaid

Christine and Diana have known each other since they were nine. In the 4th grade, Diana returned from Christmas vacation and found out that Christine had taken over her desk next to all of her friends. Despite the rough start, they are the best of friends.

David Rooney
Groomsman

Dave and Ciarán met while doing the same course and they started the Snowboarding Club at DCU. They became close friends and lived together for years. They have used having the same last name to their advantage over the years, with people often mistaking them for brothers.

Courtney Schroedel
Bridesmaid

Courtney and Diana met in kindergarten and from day one Courney has always been someone Diana could count on - wherever they go, hilarity ensues. Through thick and thin they have remained the best of friends for the last 25 years.

Mark Kiernan
Groomsman

Mark and Ciarán met while doing the same course at DCU, where they spent a lot of time snowboarding and arguing with Al. Since they first met Mark has always been one of Ciarán's closest friends and they even went on to work together after University.

Eliza Rafferty
Bridesmaid

Eliza met Diana during their first year at Northeastern University when they were living in the quiet hall despite neither of them being very quiet. They went on to live together for two more years and spent some time travelling around Europe together.

Alan O'Conor
Groomsman

Al and Ciarán met doing the same course at DCU. Despite Al always being wrong, their common love of the internet meant that they would become great friends. Al has left Dublin and is now living the dream in San Fransisco.

Lisa D'Ambrosio
Bridesmaid

Lisa and Diana met in high school, and survived the awkward years in the same tight-knit group of friends. Lisa was in Dublin the night Diana and Ciaran reunited in The Porterhouse. All of the Bulmers had nothing to do with her instant love of "Frody", her own take on Ciaran's nickname.

Russell O'Connor
Groomsman

Russell is one of Ciarán's closest friends, they met in DCU after they both became Student Union Reps in their first year. Russell ran the Snowboarding Club with Ciarán and together they organized some of the best parties DCU has ever seen.

Roxy Kripp
Flower Girl

Roxy is Diana's cousin and has been taking her role as Flower Girl very seriously with lots of practising so far. She is a master at the curtsey, so please look out for it when she reaches the top of the aisle on our wedding day.

Benjamin Fox
Ring Bearer

Ben is Diana's good friend's son and this might be the only wedding where the seven year old ring bearer is almost as tall as the bride. Ben is hoping his front teeth grow back in time for all of the wedding photos.
