Travelling from the Hilton Garden Melville

Exit the Hilton Garden Inn turning right to take the Round Swamp Road north to West Jericho Turnpike. At the traffic light turn left onto West Jericho Turnpike and continue onto Jericho Turnpike (25). After 1.7 miles turn right onto South Woods Road. The Woodlands entrance is the first left at the lights.

Travelling from JFK International Airport

Exit JFK International Airport to Belt Parkway East. Continue on the Belt Parkway to the Cross Island Expressway North to the Grand Central Parkway. The Grand Central Parkway becomes the Northern State Parkway. Stay on the NSP to Exit 36B Seaford - Oyster Bay Expressway (135). Upon accessing the 135 stay in the right lane. Second exit is Jericho Turnpike (25) (exit 14 East). Proceed down exit ramp to traffic light. Make a right on Jericho Turnpike. After half a mile turn left onto South Woods Road. The Woodlands entrance is the first left at the lights.
