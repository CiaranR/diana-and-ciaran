Traveling from the Hilton Garden Inn, Melville

Exit the Hilton Garden Inn turning right to take the Round Swamp Road north to West Jericho Turnpike. At the traffic light turn left onto West Jericho Turnpike and continue onto Jericho Turnpike (Route 25). After 1.7 miles turn right onto South Woods Road. The Woodlands entrance is the first left at the lights into the Town of Oyster Bay Golf Course. Follow the road to the Mansion where valet parking will be available.

Traveling from the West (Rockville Centre / NYC)

From the Southern State Parkway take Exit 28A North towards Syosset onto the Seaford - Oyster Bay Expressway (Route 135). Continue on Route 135 until exit 14 East for Jericho Turnpike (Route 25). Proceed down exit ramp to traffic light. Make a right on Jericho Turnpike. After half a mile turn left onto South Woods Road. The Woodlands entrance is the first left at the lights into the Town of Oyster Bay Golf Course. Follow the road to the Mansion where valet parking will be available.
