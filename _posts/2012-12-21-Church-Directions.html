Traveling from the West (NYC)

From the Southern State Parkway heading east, take exit 17 South for Hempstead Avenue South towards Malverne. Turn right onto Hempstead Avenue for and continue 1.5 miles. Turn left onto Merrick Road and follow the road under the overpass. At the lights, turn right onto Lincoln Avenue. The road will narrow, continue on and you will see Holy Trinity Lutheran Church on your right.

Traveling from the Hilton Garden Inn, Melville (Eastern Long Island)

Exit the Hilton Garden Inn turning left onto Round Swamp Road south and keep right onto the Old Country Road. Follow Old Country Road for 2 miles then merge left onto Route 135 South towards Seaford. Continue on Route 135 and take exit 4 West for the Southern State Parkway heading west. From the Southern State Parkway, take exit 17 South for Hempstead Avenue South towards Malverne. Turn right onto Hempstead Avenue and continue for 1.5 miles. Turn left onto Merrick Road and follow the road under the overpass. At the lights, turn right onto Lincoln Avenue. The road will narrow, continue on and you will see Holy Trinity Lutheran Church on your right.
